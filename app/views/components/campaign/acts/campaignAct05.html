Act 5

  1. The Slave Pens - Tag WP - Kill Overseer Krow - Go to Town
  2. Town - Talk to NPCs - Go to Control Blocks
  3. Control Blocks - Pick up Miasmeter (down/left) - Kill Justicar Casticus (top/right) - Take Eyes of Zeal - Go to Oriath Square
  4. Oriath Square - Tag WP - Open Templar Courts Entrance (top right) - Go to Templar Courts
  5. The Templar Courts - Tag WP - Go to Chamber of Innocence
  6. The Chamber of Innocence - Tag WP - Go to Sanctum of Innocence (go leftmost, then right)
  7. The Sanctum of Innocence - Kill High Templar Avarius and Innocence - Go to Chamber of Innocence
  8. Chamber of Innocence - Go to Torched Courts
  9. Torched Courts - Go to Ruined Square
  10. Ruined Square - Tag WP (top left) - Go to Ossuary (near WP)
  11. Ossuary - Pick up Sign of Purity - TP to Town
  12. Town - Talk to NPCs - WP to Ruined Square
  13. Ruined Square - Go to Reliquary (down)
  14. Reliquary - Tag WP - Collect Tukohama's Tooth, Hinekora's Hair and Valako's Jaw (corners of zone) - TP to Town
  15. Town - Talk to NPCs - WP to Ruined Square
  16. Ruined Square - Go to Cathedral Rooftop (left)
  17. Cathedral Rooftop - Go to Cathedral Apex
  18. Cathedral Apex - Activate Cradle of Purity - Kill Kitava and Kitava's Heart - Get teleported to Town
  19. Town - Sail to Act VI
