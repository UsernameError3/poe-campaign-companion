Act 7

  1. Town - Go to Broken Bridge
  2. Broken Bridge - Go to Crossroads (follow road/left)
  3. Crossroads - Tag WP (follow road) - Go to Fellshrine Ruins (down/right)
  4. Fellshrine Ruins - Go to Crypt (follow road then right)
  5. The Crypt Level 1 - Tag WP - Once per league:Solve Trial - Open Sarcophagus - Go to Crypt Level 2
  6. The Crypt Level 2 - Take Maligaro's Map - TP to Town
  7. Town - WP to Crossroads
  8. Crossroads - Go to Chamber of Sins (left)
  9. Chamber of Sins Level 1 - Place Maligaro's Map into the Map Device - Start Map - Go to Maligaro's Sanctum
  10. Maligaro's Sanctum - Kill Maligaro, Black Death and Fidelitas - Take Black Venom - TP to Chamber of Sins
  11. Chamber of Sins Level 1 - Talk to Silk for Obsidian Key - Go to Chamber of Sins Level 2 (direction of WP)
  12. Chamber of Sins Level 2 - Once per league:Solve Trial - Open Secret Passage (top/right) - Go to Den
  13. Den - Tag WP - Go to Ashen Fields (top/right)
  14. The Ashen Fields - Tag WP - Go to Fortress Encampment (follow road to the left/down)
  15. The Fortress Encampment - Kill Greust - Go to Northern Forest
  16. Northern Forest - Tag WP - Go to Dread Thicket (left/down)
  17. Dread Thicket - Collect 7 Fireflies - Go to Den of Despair (center) - Kill Gruthkul - TP to Town
  18. Town - Talk to NPCs - WP to Northern Forest
  19. Northern Forest - Go to Causeway (top right/top left)
  20. The Causeway - Tag WP (end of area) - Take Kishara's Star - Go to Vaal City
  21. Vaal City - Tag WP (middle of zone) - Talk to Yeena - Go to The Temple of Decay Level 1
  22. The Temple of Decay Level 1 - Go to The Temple of Decay Level 2
  23. The Temple of Decay Level 2 - Kill Arakaali - Go to Act VIII
  24. TP to Act 7 Town - Talk to Weylam for reward
