Act 4

  1. Aqueduct - Go to Town
  2. Town - Go to Dried Lake (bottom-left exit of Town)
  3. Dried Lake - Kill Voll - Take Deshret's Banner - TP to Town
  4. Town - Open Deshret's Seal (near Niko) - Go to Mines
  5. The Mines Level 1 - Go to Mines Level 2
  6. The Mines Level 2 - Tag Deshret's Spirit - Go to Crystal Veins
  7. Crystal Veins - Tag WP - Go to Kaom's Dream
  8. Kaom's Dream - Go to Kaom's Stronghold
  9. Kaom's Stronghold - Tag WP - Kill King Kaom - Take The Eye of Fury - TP to Town - WP to Crystal Veins
  10. Crystal Veins - Talk to Dialla - Go to Daresso's Dream
  11. Daresso's Dream - Kill Barkhul - Go to Grand Arena
  12. Grand Arena - Tag WP - Kill Unique Trio - Go to Ring of Blades - Kill Daresso - Take The Eye of Desire - TP to Town
  13. Town - Talk to NPCs - WP to Crystal Veins
  14. Crystal Veins - Talk to Dialla - Go to Belly of the Beast
  15. The Belly of the Beast Level 1 - Go to Belly of the Beast Level 2
  16. The Belly of the Beast Level 2 - Kill Piety - Go to Harvest
  17. Harvest - Tag WP - Talk to Piety - Click Black Core - Run to the 3 Arenas:
    1. Kill Shavronne - Take Malachai's Entrails
    2. Kill Maligaro - Take Malachai's Heart
    3. Kill Doedre - Take Malachai's Lungs
  18. Harvest - Talk to Piety - Go to Black Core - Kill Piety and Malachai - Go to Black Heart
  19. Black Heart - Kill Malachai and all 3 Heart of the Beast - TP to Town
  20. Town - Talk to NPCs - Go to Ascent
  21. The Ascent - Walk to the summit - Activate Lever - Go to Act V
