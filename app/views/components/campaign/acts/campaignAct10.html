Act 10

Warning: After you kill Kitava, you will have -60% to all resistances, making all combat harder if you don't have gear with sufficient resistances. Therefore you might want to level up at The Blood Aqueduct in Act 9, or The Ravaged Square in Act 10, till you reach around level 70, and then kill Kitava.

  1. Oriath Docks - Go to Cathedral Rooftop
  2. Cathedral Rooftop - Go to Cathedral Apex (left) - Kill Plaguewing - Talk to Bannon - Go to Ravaged Square (top/right)
  3. The Ravaged Square - Tag WP (right/up) - Go to Torched Courts (right from WP)
  4. Torched Courts - Go to Desecrated Chambers
  5. The Desecrated Chambers - Tag WP – Go to Sanctum of Innocence (go leftmost, then right)
  6. Sanctum of Innocence - Kill Avarius – Take The Staff of Purity - TP to Town
  7. Town - Talk to NPCs - WP to Ravaged Square
  8. Ravaged Square - Go to Ossuary (near WP) - Solve Trial - TP to Town
  9. Ravaged Square - Go to Control Blocks (down)
  10. Control Blocks - Tag WP (left of entrance) - Kill Vilenta (follow right-hand wall) - TP to Town
  11. Town - Talk to NPCs - WP to Ravaged Square
  12. Once per league:Ravaged Square - Go to Ossuary (next to WP) - Go to Bone Pits - Solve Trial - TP to Town - WP to Ravaged Square
  13. Ravaged Square - Talk to Innocence (right from WP) - Go to Canals
  14. The Canals - Go to Feeding Trough (follow canal)
  15. The Feeding Trough – Go to Altar of Hunger (top)
  16. Altar of Hunger - Kill Kitava - Go to Oriath - Talk to Lani for skill point
