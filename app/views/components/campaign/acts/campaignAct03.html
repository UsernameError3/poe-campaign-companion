Act 3

  1. The City of Sarn - Tag WP - Kill Guard Captain - Talk to Clarissa - Go to Town
  2. Town - Go to Slums
  3. Slums - Go to Crematorium
  4. Crematorium - Tag WP - Once per league:Solve Trial - Kill Piety - Get Tolman's Bracelet - TP to Town
  5. Town - Get Sewer Keys from Clarissa - Go to Slums
  6. Slums - Open Sewer Grating (top) - Go to Sewers
  7. The Sewers - Take Bust of Gaius Sentari - Tag WP - Take Bust of Hector Titucius and Bust of Marceus Lioneye - Go to Marketplace (top)
  8. Marketplace - Tag WP - Once per league:Go to Catacombs (near WP) - Solve Trial - TP to Town
  9. Town - Talk to NPCs - WP to Marketplace - Go to Battlefront (up/left)
  10. Battlefront - Tag WP - Kill Aurelianus - Take Ribbon Spool - Go to Docks (left)
  11. Docks - Tag WP (near Fairgraves) - Take Thaumetic Sulphite (near WP) - TP to Town - WP to Battlefront
  12. Battlefront - Go to Solaris Temple (up)
  13. Solaris Temple Level 1 - Tag WP - Go to Solaris Temple Level 2
  14. Solaris Temple Level 2 - Tag WP - Talk to Dialla for Infernal Talc - WP to Sewers
  15. Sewers - Open Undying Blockage - Go to Ebony Barracks
  16. Ebony Barracks - Tag WP - Kill General Gravicius (up) - Go to Lunaris Temple (up)
  17. Lunaris Temple Level 1 - Tag WP - Go to Lunaris Temple Level 2
  18. Lunaris Temple Level 2 - Kill Piety - Take Tower Key - TP to Town
  19. Town - Talk to NPCs - WP to Ebony Barracks
  20. Ebony Barracks - Go to Imperial Gardens (right/top right)
  21. Imperial Gardens - Tag WP - Once per league:Solve Trial (top) - Open Locked Room (top/right) - Go to Sceptre of God
  22. Sceptre of God - Go to Upper Sceptre of God
  23. Upper Sceptre of God - Go to Tower Rooftop
  24. Tower Rooftop - Kill Dominus and Dominus, Ascendant - Go to Act IV

All Skill Gems

If players require skill gems for their build, they can purchase it from Siosa at The Library:

  1. TP to Imperial Gardens - Go to Library (top-left)
  2. The Library - Tag WP (near Siosa) - Talk to Siosa - Activate Loose Candle (top-left/bottom-left of area) - Go to Archives
  3. The Archives - Find 4 Golden Pages - Go to Library
  4. Library - Talk to Siosa - Purchase Skill Gems
  5. If certain skill gems are unavailable at Siosa, then players must wait for Act 7 after which Lilly Roth offers all gems in the game
