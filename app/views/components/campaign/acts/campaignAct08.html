Act 8

  1. The Sarn Ramparts - Go to Sarn Encampment
  2. Sarn Encampment - Go to Toxic Conduits
  3. The Toxic Conduits - Open Loose Gate (end of area) - Go to Doedre's Cesspool
  4. Doedre's Cesspool - Go to Cauldron - Activate Valve - Kill Deodre - Tag WP - Go to Grand Promenade (down/left from WP)
  5. The Grand Promenade - Go to Bath House (follow right-hand fence)
  6. The Bath House - Tag WP - Kill Hector Titucius - Take Wings of Vastiri - Once per league:Solve Trial (top or left) - Go to High Gardens (top or left)
  7. High Gardens – Go to Pools of Terror (right) - Kill Yugul - TP to Town
  8. Town - Talk to NPCs - WP to Bath House
  9. Bath House - Go to Lunaris Concourse (right)
  10. The Lunaris Concourse - Tag WP - Go to Lunaris Temple (top)
  11. Lunaris Temple Level 1 – Tag WP - Go to Lunaris Temple Level 2
  12. Lunaris Temple Level 2 - Kill Dusk - Take Moon Orb - TP to Town
  13. Town - Talk to NPCs - WP to Toxic Conduits
  14. Toxic Conduits - Go to Quay (up/right from WP)
  15. The Quay - Take Ankh of Eternity (down) - Go to Resurrection Site (down/left) - Kill Tolman - Talk to Clarissa (nearby) – Go to Grain Gate (up/right)
  16. The Grain Gate - Tag WP - Kill Gemling Legion - Go to Imperial Fields
  17. The Imperial Fields - Tag WP (follow road up) - Go to Solaris Temple (follow road then further up)
  18. Solaris Temple Level 1 - Tag WP - Go to Solaris Temple Level 2 (up from WP)
  19. Solaris Temple Level 2 - Kill Dawn - Take Sun Orb - TP to Town
  20. Town - Talk to NPCs - WP to Lunaris Concourse
  21. Lunaris Concourse - Go to Harbour Bridge (down/right)
  22. Harbour Bridge - Go to Sky Shrine (middle of bridge) - Activate Statue of the Sisters - Kill Lunaris and Solaris - Go to Act IX
