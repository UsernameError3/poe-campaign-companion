Act 6

  1. Town - Go to Twilight Strand
  2. Twilight Strand - Kill all Monsters (for all skill gems from Lilly Roth) - TP to Town
  3. Town - Talk to NPCs - Go to Coast
  4. Coast - Go to Mudflats
  5. Mud Flats - Kill Dishonoured Queen - Take Eye of Conquest - Go to Karui Fortress (top/left)
  6. The Karui Fortress - Kill Tukohama (right) - Go to Ridge (outside of Arena and right)
  7. The Ridge - Tag WP - Go to Lower Prison
  8. Lower Prison - Tag WP - Once per league:Solve Trial - Go to Shavronne's Tower
  9. Shavronne's Tower - Go to Prison Rooftop
  10. Prison Rooftop - Kill Brutus and Shavronne - Go to Warden's Chambers
  11. Warden's Chambers - Go to Prisoner's Gate
  12. Prisoner's Gate - Tag WP - Go to Valley of the Fire Drinker
  13. Valley of the Fire Drinker - Kill Abberath - Go back to Prisoner's Gate
  14. Prisoner's Gate - Go to Western Forest
  15. Western Forest - Tag WP (follow road) - Go to Riverways (follow road)
  16. Riverways - Tag WP - Go to Wetlands (up/left)
  17. Wetlands - Go to Spawning Ground
  18. Spawning Ground - Destroy 3 Ryslatha's Nests - Kill Ryslatha - TP to Town
  19. Town - Talk to NPCs - WP to Riverways
  20. Riverways - Go to Southern Forest (follow road, then down/right)
  21. Southern Forest - Tag WP (end of area) - Go to Cavern of Anger (near WP)
  22. Cavern of Anger - Take The Black Flag - Go to Beacon
  23. Beacon - Tag WP - Refuel the Beacon - Light the Beacon - Talk to Weylam Roth - Sail to Brine King's Reef
  24. The Brine King's Reef - Tag WP - Go to The Brine King's Throne - Kill Tsoagoth - Sail to Act VII
